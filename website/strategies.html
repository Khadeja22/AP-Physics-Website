Strategies You Need to Know!




How to Approach MCQ

  1. Solve the problem before looking at the answer choices
  2. Manipulate equations/formulas before working in with real values
  3. Focus on principles rather than values
  4. Use POE (Process of Elimination)

How to Approach FRQ

  1. Draw full FBD (Free-Body Diagrams)
  2. Pick out relevant equations from the AP Reference Sheet
  3. Check for multiple cases
  4. Use correct vocabulary

Time-Conciousness

It is very important to keep time into consideration throughout the test. For each FRQ, you will get approximately 22 minutes, which is very little. Use the strategies above to have super-sonic speed. Brain-dump as soon as you read the question then go through what you just thought and draw a star next to the relevant details you though of. Use those to solve the problems.
